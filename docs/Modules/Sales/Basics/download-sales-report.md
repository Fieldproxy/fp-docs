## Save Data To Your Local 

Downloading your team's sales records is as easy as clicking a couple buttons. Literally.

- Login to **[Fieldproxy](https://manager.fieldproxy.com/)**
- In the Dashboard view that appears, you can find every single piece of sales metric that your team has recorded

![Sales Proxy Dashboard](https://fpobstore.s3.sng01.cloud-object-storage.appdomain.cloud/1633067149749SP.PNG)

- Click on the **Reports** screen.

![Reports Screen - Fieldproxy](https://fpobstore.s3.sng01.cloud-object-storage.appdomain.cloud/1633067284064Reports.PNG)

- To download the report that you've need, just click on it from the list of options available.

![Reports Screen - Fieldproxy](https://fpobstore.s3.sng01.cloud-object-storage.appdomain.cloud/1633067395411reports1.PNG)

- Select the desired date range.

![Date Range - Fieldproxy](https://fpobstore.s3.sng01.cloud-object-storage.appdomain.cloud/1633067472906download.PNG)

- Click on the little green cloud icon to start the download.

![Download Button - Fieldproxy](https://fpobstore.s3.sng01.cloud-object-storage.appdomain.cloud/1633067554799downloaded.PNG)


And just like that, your attendance report is downloaded as a local copy.


Hope this helps!
